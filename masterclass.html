Masterclass
The Superhuman OS

A cohort-based intensive for founders and leaders who want to build internal order and external leverage—without losing what matters most.

Most high-performers don't fail from incompetence. They fracture from carrying complexity without a system that can hold it.

The Superhuman OS Masterclass is an immersive, cohort-based experience built on the framework I've developed over two decades of coaching, ministry, systems architecture, and real-world operator experience. It's not a course. It's a formation environment.

Over the course of the program, you'll build your own Superhuman OS—a personalized operating system for making decisions, managing energy, architecting your business, and orienting your life around what actually matters.

Who It's For
This is not for everyone.

The Superhuman OS Masterclass is designed for founders doing $500K–$5M, executives navigating growth or transition, coaches and consultants who are effective with clients but struggling to apply their own frameworks, and leaders who are tired of performing competence while privately managing chaos.

You should not apply if you're looking for hype, shortcuts, or someone to tell you you're amazing. This is formation work. It's honest, it's rigorous, and it changes things.
